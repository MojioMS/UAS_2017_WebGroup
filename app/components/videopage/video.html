{{videoContent}} Analysis for Water Flow Dynamics in Aa River:
Water flow dynamics of a river has major influence in the ecological functions of the river. An intricate relationship exists between water flow dynamics and sedimentation. These processes influence the prevalence of vegetation in the river which is a key part of river naturalization process.

River velocity, a crucial measure of water flow dynamics is also an indicator of effectivity of river naturalization process. In this project, we utilize video captured from UAV platform to measure the velocity of the river. We deployed three floats on the river and captured the video as the floats drifted along the river. The videos were captured with Sony A5100 in visible spectrum and with FLIR Ocean Scout TK in thermal spectrum.
